Artwork Finder

Apple Music Artwork Finder

Search for album artwork by text, or paste an Apple Music URL to find high-resolution artwork for albums, playlists, stations, artists, music videos, and curators.

Text search is limited to albums. URL search works with albums, playlists, stations, artists, music videos, and curators from Apple Music.

How to Use

Search by album or artist name to browse album artwork, or paste an Apple Music URL to fetch additional artwork and metadata. URL search can currently retrieve artwork for albums, playlists, stations, artists, music videos, and curators.

For example, you can use album URLs such as Pray For The Wicked, playlist URLs such as Easy Hits, station URLs such as Charting Now Radio, artist URLs such as Paper Idol, music video URLs such as Cake by the Ocean, and curator URLs such as Rap Life Radio with Ebro Darden.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does a search sometimes fail?
Most public Apple Music URLs should work, but deeply personalised or private items may not be available through Apple’s public data. Content blockers can also prevent this page from communicating with Apple’s servers.

Can I use your website with automation tools?
I'd prefer you didn't as it will likely lead to your browser being blocked. If you have automation requirements, take a look at my Apple Music Artwork Shortcut which can be used within Apple Shortcuts to fetch artwork URLs directly.
