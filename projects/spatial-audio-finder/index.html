Spatial Audio Finder

My iOS app, Music Library Tracker, monitors changes to your Apple Music or iTunes Match library and sends you notifications when your tracks are changed (i.e. if Apple delete them). A future update coming soon will also be able to tell you when your tracks are upgraded to Spatial Audio! Download it now on the App Store.

Follow @NewSpatialAudio on Twitter to see when new albums and tracks with Spatial Audio are released or old tracks get upgraded to Spatial Audio.

Want to say thanks for this tool? Sign up to my free newsletter which will give you exclusive updates about the Spatial Audio Finder as well as early access to any related apps.

This tool is designed to help you find which tracks are available in Spatial Audio / Dolby Atmos on Apple Music. Just enter an artist name and press search to retrieve a list of albums featuring that artist which contain Spatial Audio tracks.

Searching for Spatial Audio works thanks to a complex database I've built up which constantly scans the Apple Music service and recognises when tracks get upgraded. I also send alerts of new tracks and albums to the @NewSpatialAudio account on Twitter. A future update to my Music Library Tracker app will allow it to scan your library and notify you when your music gets upgraded!
