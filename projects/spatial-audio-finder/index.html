How to Use

This tool is designed to help you find which tracks are available in Spatial Audio on Apple Music, including head-tracked Dolby Atmos and fixed-position Dolby Audio. Enter an artist name and press search to retrieve matching artists, then choose the artist you want to see their upgraded tracks.

Searching works thanks to a database I built that scans Apple Music and recognises when tracks get upgraded. If an artist does not currently have any Spatial Audio tracks, they can still be added to the monitoring system for future upgrades.

Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as Spatial Audio?
This finder includes Apple Music tracks marked as Spatial Audio, including Dolby Atmos and Dolby Audio releases.

Why does an artist show no results?
They may not currently have any tracks upgraded to Spatial Audio, or the database may not have discovered their catalogue yet. Searches can help expand the monitoring system over time.

Can I check my own library?
Yes. Music Library Tracker can scan the music library on your iPhone or iPad and help you find Spatial Audio upgrades in music you already own or have added from Apple Music.
