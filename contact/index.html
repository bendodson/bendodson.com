Contracts / Projects

I am currently available from February 2017 for a short (less than 3 months) contract or project. I'm only interested in remote working opportunities and my day rate is £600 (plus VAT if applicable). App Ideas

I get a lot of emails about app ideas due to an article I wrote about pitching app ideas back in 2009. I have had over 900 apps pitched to me since that time and nearly all of them have been poorly thought out with an assumption that an idea is actually worth something; the harsh reality is that an idea is worth absolutely nothing. If you have a wealth of experience in a certain market, you have contacts, and you have a good idea that hasn't been done before, then get in touch and I'll see what I can do (although I'm not interested in equity). If you are just a person with 'the next greatest app idea' and nothing else, then I'm not interested.
