Run your 5K plan while watching whatever you like

Chaise Longue to 5K is a Couch to 5K app built for people who want the run plan without the fuss. It gives you clear timing for each stage of your run, syncs progress across your devices, and uses Picture in Picture so the timer can stay visible while you watch something else.

It started as an Apple TV app for treadmill running, then grew into a universal app for iPhone, iPad, Apple TV, and Mac. Buy it once and use it wherever you run.

Built Around Picture in Picture

The app was designed first for Apple TV so you could keep the run timing visible while watching something else. The same Picture in Picture approach works across iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Privacy

Chaise Longue to 5K does not require an internet connection and contains no advertising, no tracking, and no in-app purchases. Run progress is synced privately via iCloud.
