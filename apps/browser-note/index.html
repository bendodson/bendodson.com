Put a pause between you and bad browsing habits

The web is full of places you open without thinking. Browser Note lets you add reminders and notes directly to websites so you can pause before doomscrolling, ordering takeaway, checking social media, or making a purchase you already promised yourself you would avoid.

It works as a Safari extension across iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Set a note for a single page or an entire domain, choose whether snoozing is allowed, and let iCloud keep everything in sync.

Seamless Syncing

Browser Note runs on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. It includes text and video tutorials for installing and using the Safari extension, multiple font sizes, and a customisable snooze timer.

Privacy

Browser Note does not require an internet connection and contains no advertising, no tracking, no analytics, and no in-app purchases. Your notes stay in your iCloud account so they can sync to your devices.
