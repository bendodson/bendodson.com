Privacy Policy

I respect your privacy. For that reason, Browser Note doesn't use your data or try and gain information about you via analytics. If you are signed into an Apple account, your notes will be stored within iCloud so they can be synced to all of your devices. Other than that, nothing goes to any servers owned by 3rd parties or Dodo Apps. The app does not require an internet connection.

Browser Note contains no advertising, tracking, analytics, or in-app purchases.
