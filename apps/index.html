Music Library Tracker

Automatic monitoring for your Apple Music library

iPhone and iPad

Music Library Tracker keeps an eye on your music library and monitors additions, deletions, metadata changes, and Spatial Audio upgrades. It was originally built after I realised streaming libraries can change quietly in the background, and has since become a tool for people who care deeply about their music collections.

One-time download cost of $3.99

Browser Note

Add reminders and notes to websites

iPhone, iPad, and Mac

Browser Note is a Safari extension that lets you add a pause to your browsing. It can show reminders on specific pages or entire domains. Whether you are trying to avoid social media, think twice before ordering takeaway, or just need to leave a reminder on a particular webpage, this is an essential app.

One-time download cost of $1.99

Chaise Longue to 5K

After all, couches are so common

iPhone, iPad, Apple TV, and Mac

Chaise Longue to 5K helps you build up to a 5K over nine weeks with Picture in Picture support so you can keep the run timer visible while watching video or using other apps. I originally built it for using on Apple TV with my treadmill before porting it to iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

One-time download cost of $3.99

Return to Dark Tower Assistant

A companion app for Return to Dark Tower

iPhone and iPad

Return to Dark Tower Assistant tracks phases, actions, hero boards, cards, and advantages for the Return to Dark Tower board game. It is optimised for iPad Slide Over so it can sit on top of the official companion app during a game.
